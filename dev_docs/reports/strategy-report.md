# Investment Strategy Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

This report evaluates various investment strategies over a 15-year window with a focus on vehicle deployment and contribution modes. The analysis aims to identify robust patterns for long-term wealth building using fewer vs. more tickers and front-loaded vs. continuous contributions. The top-performing strategy, "v1-front5-per_winner," achieved a median CAGR of 20.25% and a median gain of 1489.27%, with a 100% win rate across all tested windows.

## What Patterns Are Robust

- **High Win Rate**: All top scenarios maintained a 100% win rate across the 7 valid windows.
- **High Median Gains**: The top strategies consistently showed high median gains, with the best scenario reaching a median gain of 1489.27%.
- **Significant Drawdowns**: Median max drawdowns were substantial, ranging from 42.89% to 51.13%, indicating potential volatility.

## Fewer vs More Tickers

- **Single Vehicle (v1)**: The top two strategies used a single vehicle per year, achieving the highest median CAGR of 20.25%.
- **Multiple Vehicles (v2-v4)**: While strategies using more vehicles (up to 4) showed slightly lower median CAGRs (around 18.08% to 18.49%), they still maintained high gains and win rates.

## Front-Loaded vs Continuous Contributions

- **Front-Loaded (front5)**: Dominated the top ranks, with the highest median gains and CAGRs, suggesting effectiveness in maximizing returns.
- **Continuous**: Ranked lower, with a median CAGR of 14.80% and median gain of 693.01%, indicating less effectiveness compared to front-loaded strategies.

## Recommended Default Configuration

- **Vehicles Per Year**: 1
- **Deployment**: Front-loaded (front5)
- **Contribution Mode**: Per winner

## Risk Notes

- **Drawdown Risk**: High drawdowns (up to 51.13%) suggest significant risk, requiring careful risk management.
- **Volatility**: Strategies with higher returns also exhibited higher volatility, necessitating a balanced approach.

## Next Tests

- **Sensitivity Analysis**: Explore the impact of varying the number of vehicles and contribution modes on performance.
- **Risk Mitigation Strategies**: Investigate methods to reduce drawdowns while maintaining high returns.
- **Longer Time Horizons**: Test scenarios beyond the 15-year window to assess long-term sustainability.

This analysis provides a foundation for selecting robust investment strategies, emphasizing the benefits of front-loaded contributions and the use of fewer vehicles for maximizing long-term gains.
